Asbuton Soft Rocks

Definition : A high quality hard type Buton asphalt product originating from the Kabungka Mining License. This product, which is intended for export and local markets, has a production capacity of up to 400,000 tons per year.


Advantages : (1) Has more Asphalten content higher than Asphalt Oil (2) Natural Asphalt is equivalent to the Gilsonite harder than petroleum asphalt (3) The mineral content of CaCO3 and natural asphalt can increase the stiffness of Hotmix in Hotmix's comparison with oil asphalt


Function & uses : As a raw material for making additives to improve the quality of hot mix in heavy traffic (toll roads and national roads)

  • Bitumen Content : 20-26%
  • Water Content : <16%
  • PEN : 40-60



Asbuton Hard Rocks

Definition : It is a high quality soft type Buton asphalt product originating from IUP Lawele


Advantages : (1)Contains Resin and Malten which function as Anti-Stripping in comparison Oil Asphalt (2) The composition of Natural Asphalt is equivalent to Asphalt from Trinidad Lake Asphalt (TLA– Tobago USA) (3) Equivalent to Polymer Modified Asphalt (PMB PG 70)


Function & Uses : As a substitute raw material for oil asphalt pen 60/70 for medium traffic such as provincial roads and district roads.

  • Bitumen Content : 18-24%
  • Water Content : <16%
  • PEN : 5-10
